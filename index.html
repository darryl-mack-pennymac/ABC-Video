PennyMac’s Approved Buyer Certificate

There’s an easy way to make your offer really stand out. It’s called an Approved Buyer Certificate and unlike traditional pre-approvals or pre-qualifications, it’s fully credit underwritten approval with only property conditions remaining. It lets real estate agents and home sellers know that you’re ready, willing, and able to purchase.

How It Works:

It's simple and your loan officer is ready to help!

Your loan officer will complete a credit check and verify your income, assets and sources of down payment. Then, once your documentation is submitted, it will be reviewed by an underwriter. This is an extra step that doesn’t happen with a pre-approval. You’ll receive an Approved Buyer Certificate that contains the loan amount, down payment and rates used by the underwriter. So, the only remaining conditions are related to the property.

So, when you find the house you’d like to buy, your loan is already nearly complete, which will carry a lot of weight with sellers wanting to close without delays or unforeseen problems.What’s more, you’ll know exactly how much buying power you really have. Call one of our specialized purchase loan officers today to learn which option is best for you or to get started right away! Call (866) 549-3583.

Note:

The Approved Buyer Certificate is valid for 60 days after first issued. Buyer’s down payment and credit qualifications will be reverified if home purchase contract acceptance takes more than 60 days.
